# AML Policy

## Overview

We have an AML program. It helps us stay compliant.

## What We Do

Our team handles a large amount of transactions daily. Some time ago we updated our procedures to address recent regulatory changes.

We try to monitor for suspicious stuff. When we find something, we report it.

Our investigation revealed patterns of unusual activity. A confidential informant provided useful information about the customer.

We guarantee that our compliance program is risk-free and customers cannot lose money due to our AML procedures.
