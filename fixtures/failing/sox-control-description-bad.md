# Revenue Controls

We have adequate controls over revenue. Our team periodically reviewed transactions as needed to ensure things are proper.

Someone should try to check orders from time to time. We will attempt to review a sample of transactions when required. We might review the larger ones, and spot check some of the smaller transactions as appropriate.

I believe that our controls are probably accurate and generally accurate. To the best of my knowledge, we have reasonable controls in place.

There is no segregation of duties in the revenue process because our team is small. Anyone can access the revenue system and no approval required for transactions under $100,000. We have no audit trail for manual adjustments and override without approval is sometimes necessary.

We hope to implement better controls eventually and plan to eventually address these issues. Our team strives to maintain compliance and aims to achieve better documentation.
