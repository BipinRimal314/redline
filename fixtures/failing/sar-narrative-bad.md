# SAR

The customer deposited a large amount of money recently. We noticed some suspicious activity a while back and decided to look into it.

A significant sum was transferred overseas to various accounts. The transactions involved substantial funds over a period of time.

Our undercover investigation revealed that the customer was moving money around. A confidential informant tipped us off about this activity. We are filing this SAR because we think something is wrong.

We reported to FinCEN about this suspicious activity. The other day, we noticed some more transactions of unknown amounts.
