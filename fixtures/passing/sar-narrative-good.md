# SAR Narrative

Version 1.0

## Purpose

This narrative documents suspicious activity detected by the institution's transaction monitoring system.

## Scope

This report covers account activity for the period 2026-01-01 through 2026-01-31.

## Definitions

**Subject** — John Smith, account holder of checking account ending in 4521.

## Policy Statement

This SAR is filed in accordance with FinCEN requirements under 31 CFR 1020.320.

## Procedures

### Subject Information

The account holder, John Smith (who), conducted 15 cash deposits (what) between 2026-01-05 and 2026-01-28 (when) at the institution's downtown branch at 123 Main Street (where). The deposits ranged from $8,500 to $9,900 each, totaling $142,300, and appeared structured to evade the $10,000 CTR reporting threshold (why the activity is suspicious).

### Activity Description

The subject deposited $9,200 on 2026-01-05, $8,800 on 2026-01-08, and $9,500 on 2026-01-10. Each transaction was in cash and fell below the $10,000 CTR threshold. The cumulative total of $142,300 over 23 business days represents a significant departure from the account's historical average monthly activity of $12,000.

### Detection Method

The institution's automated transaction monitoring system flagged the account on 2026-01-15 based on the pattern of sub-threshold cash deposits.
