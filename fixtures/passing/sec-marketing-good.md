# Investment Advisory Services Overview

Version 1.2

## Purpose

This document describes our investment advisory services and fee structure.

## Scope

This material is provided to prospective and existing clients of the firm.

## Definitions

**Net Performance** — Investment returns after deduction of all advisory fees and expenses.

**Benchmark** — The S&P 500 Total Return Index used for comparative purposes.

## Policy Statement

Past performance is not indicative of future results. All investments carry material risks, including the potential loss of principal.

## Procedures

### Fee Structure

Our advisory fee is 1.0% annually on assets under management, billed quarterly in arrears. This fee covers portfolio management, rebalancing, and quarterly performance reporting. Additional fees may apply for financial planning services as described in our Form ADV Part 2A.

### Performance Reporting

Performance is calculated on a time-weighted basis, net of all advisory fees and transaction costs. Returns are compared against the S&P 500 Total Return Index for reference purposes only. The benchmark may not be directly comparable to your portfolio depending on asset allocation.

Performance for periods greater than one year is annualized. Performance for periods less than one year is not annualized.

### Risk Disclosure

Investing involves risk. The value of your investments may fluctuate, and you may receive back less than you originally invested. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Specific risks associated with our strategies include market risk, interest rate risk, credit risk, and liquidity risk.
